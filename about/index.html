about me

Hi! I'm Rose. I'm a first year CS student at the University of Bristol, and I'm in love with Rust. I want it to take over the world some day. When I'm not fangirling over Rust, I'm usually complaining about something else, but don't let that fool you. A lot of stuff is actually quite cool, in my opinion.

I have a few hobby projects:

  • kerux, a matrix server
  • kernel, a kernel targeting aarch64 - pending name change :D
  • boolean-parser, a program for me and a friend to learn Haskell the fun way
  • logic-graph + logicism, a combinatorial logic simulator and its ui
